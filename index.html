About us

  • The mission of the Navayuvaka Kalavrinda Library is to provide students with basic infrastructure facility for self- study by providing them books related to all educational fields.
  • The Navayuvaka Kalavrinda Library is a unique organization dedicated to students wanting to study.
  • It is a Self Study Centre with Reference books on all major educational facilities available in India.
  • Navayuvaka Kalavrinda Library provides a unique opportunity to students to be in contact with the latest developments of education in India
  • The Navayuvaka Kalavrinda came up with the noble idea of providing a study place with facilities that makes up an environment for studying.
  • It was started on 6th September 2005. It was highly appreciated by all leading newspapers.
  • Within a short span of its start ,it has now more than 1,000 members enrolled.
  • Navayuvaka Kalavrinda library is the first Library in india which is Free and has been recognized by the Kerala library Counsel.
